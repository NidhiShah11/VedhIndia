Meet Our Creative Team

We are a team of enthusiastic, imaginative minds that love to push the limits of creativity. He also brings a valuable experience in Print Production and Packaging. Endowed with fine leadership skills and strong business acumen, His stewardship is taking Vedh India towards a new horizon of success.
